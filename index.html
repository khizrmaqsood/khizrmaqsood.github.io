    About Me

    I’m a student at Northwestern University studying manufacturing and design engineering. Growing up in Karachi, Pakistan, I was surrounded by music, culture, and art, and gaining a technical education to balance my creative side has established an outlet for my work through design. Excited by the breadth of opportunity within the umbrella term that is ‘design', I strive to use the field for aesthetic expression with roots grounded in humanity; I’d like make things that not only look good, but bring robust functionality to genuinely improve the lives of their users.

    Coming to the US has made me aware of the incredible privilege I have as an international student from a 3rd world country with access to the best possible education—I’ve promised myself I will use this privilege to better the world around me regardless of where I am. In my free time I enjoy sketching, walking outdoors, and playing the ukulele and guitar!
